What is Blood Donation?

Blood donation is a voluntary process where a healthy person donates blood to help patients during surgeries, accidents, or medical emergencies.

Why Blood Donation is Important?

Every drop counts. A single donation can save up to three lives. Blood cannot be manufactured and depends completely on donors.

Why Blockchain?

Blockchain ensures secure, tamper-proof records, complete traceability, and real-time tracking of blood from donor to patient.
